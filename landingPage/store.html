Ordering from us is simple, secure, and convenient. Here's how the process works from start to finish:

Step 1

  1. Browse our collection and click “Add to Cart” for the products you want.
  2. You can view or update your cart at any time by clicking the cart icon.

Step 2

When you're ready to checkout, click the “Make an Order” button in your cart. This will open a secure form where you'll provide important delivery details, including:

  1. Your full name, Contact number, Email address and Delivery address
  2. Preferred delivery method (Courier Guy, PAXI, or Payment on Delivery)

Step 3

After submitting your delivery details, you'll be directed to a secure payment page. We support several payment methods including debit/credit cards.

Step 4

Once your order is placed:

  1. You'll receive a confirmation email with your order summary and delivery details.
  2. If you paid online, your payment will be pending confirmation.
  3. Once confirmed, we'll begin preparing your order for dispatch.

Step 5

We'll send you updates as your order moves through our system. You'll be notified when your package is on the way.
